About me


I'm Melanie, a product designer and outdoor health enthusiast. I approach design similar to running - with passion, grit and determination. (Both are daily rituals).

I’ve spent the past 2 years practicing UX/UI design and research for startups, sharpening my project management and design skills through freelance work, and most recently working with a team of designers, PMs, and engineers as a product designer at Handy.

My philosophy

When I traveled to Copenhagen I discovered Hygge [‘HOO-gah”], which loosely translates to “coziness.” From what I understand, Hygge is all about designing your life to support happiness, good health, and wellbeing. I’m a big Hygge enthusiast and try to make a daily effort to do the things that inspire me and make me happy. This means drinking a good cup of coffee and working on projects that I believe in.
